About Us

Welcome to StringUp, your premier partner in badminton sports management. We're passionate about the game of badminton and dedicated to providing top-notch services to players and enthusiasts alike. Whether you're a professional athlete striving for the perfect game or a recreational player looking to enjoy your favorite sport, we're here to support you every step of the way.


Our Mission is Championing badminton with unparalleled support and guidance.

We understand the unique bond between a player and their racket, and we're committed to ensuring that every racket performs at its best. Our expertise lies in restringing broken rackets, breathing new life into your equipment, and helping you unleash your true potential on the court.

Meet the passionate individuals who make StringUp a reality. Our team comprises experienced badminton enthusiasts who understand the intricacies of the game and the importance of well-maintained equipment. We're here to provide expert guidance, answer your questions, and support you in reaching your badminton goals.

Sayam Bothra

Co-founder

Sayam Bothra, co-founder of StringUp began his badminton journey at age 10. He quickly rose to professional status under the guidance of coach Govardhan Reddy Sir after winning a local tournament within just six months of starting the sport. His 12-year professional career was cut short by a back injury at age 22, leading him to pivot towards computer engineering. Fascinated by technology's problem-solving potential, Sayam combined his passion for badminton with his tech expertise to conceive StringUp. This innovative solution offers players the convenience of on-demand racket restringing services, eliminating the need for time-consuming store visits and bringing professional-grade maintenance to their fingertips. Through StringUp, Sayam aims to enhance the badminton experience by leveraging technology to solve practical challenges faced by players.

Govardhan Reddy

Co-founder

Govardhan Reddy, co-founder of StringUp and former Junior National Coach, brings a wealth of experience and passion to the game. He has dedicated his career to nurturing young talent and shaping future stars of Indian badminton, including Saina Nehwal, Parupalli Kashyap, and Sai Praneeth. With a holistic coaching philosophy emphasizing physical fitness, mental resilience, and technical skills, he has successfully guided numerous junior players to national and international victories. Additionally, he helps players develop off-court strategies to maximize their potential and foster a lifelong love for badminton.
